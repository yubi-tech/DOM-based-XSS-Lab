About This Challenge

Welcome to our interactive cybersecurity lab designed to help you sharpen your skills in finding vulnerabilities! Here, you'll encounter real-world scenarios that allow you to practice various techniques, including SQL injection, DOM-based XSS, and CSRF attacks.

What You'll Learn

  • Identifying and exploiting common web vulnerabilities.
  • Understanding the implications of security flaws.
  • Applying best practices for secure coding.

How to Get Started

Start by navigating to the different pages and look for hidden vulnerabilities. Once you discover a vulnerability, follow the hints provided to exploit it and reveal the flags!

Challenge Highlights

Each page in this lab has been crafted to simulate a real-world application. Pay close attention to the feedback page, where you can test your skills with a DOM-based XSS vulnerability!
